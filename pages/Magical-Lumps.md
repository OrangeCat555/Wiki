Magical Lumps are [Magical Items](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Magical-Items) used for crafting other [Magical Items](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Magical-Items), [Magical Armor](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Magical-Armor) and [Magical Gadgets](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Magical-Gadgets).

## Obtaining Magical Lumps
### Tier 1
Tier 1 Magical Lumps can be crafted by placing an Nether Wart into the [Grind Stone](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Grind-Stone).
Each Nether Wart gives 2 Lumps.

### Tier 2
Tier 2 Magical Lumps can be crafted by placing 4 Tier 1 Magical Lumps into [Enhanced Crafting Table](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Enhanced-Crafting-Table) following the recipe in the slimefun guide.

### Tier 3
Tier 3 Magical Lumps have the same recipe as Tier 2; but instead of using tier 1 Magical Lumps, Tier 2 are used.
