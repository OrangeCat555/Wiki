Magical Book Covers are a [Magical Item](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Magical-Items) which are used in recipies such as the [Elemental Staff](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Elemental-Staves).

## Obtaining
To obtain this item, use the recipe from the Slimefun Guide, placing the items in the [Enhanced Crafting Table](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Enhanced-Crafting-Table).
