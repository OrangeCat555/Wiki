These Slimefun [Items](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Items) are simple ways to restore hearts and remove negative effects.

The Vitamins and Medicine are also able to cure radiation.

## Rag
Restores 2 Hearts 
Extinguishes Fire

## Bandage
Restores 4 Hearts
Extinguises Fire

## Splint
Restores 2 Hearts

## Vitamins
Restores 4 Hearts
Exinguises Fire
Cures Poison/Wither/Radiation

## Medicine
Restores 4 Hearts
Exinguises Fire
Cures Poison/Wither/Radiation
