This [Basic Machine](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Basic-Machines)'s purpose is to reignite the fire of a Smeltery when used.

## Obtaining the Automated Ignition Chamber
This Machine can be crafted in the [Enhanced Crafting Table](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Enhanced-Crafting-Table).
Once it is crafted, place it directly next to the dispenser and fill it with Flint and Steels for it to work.
