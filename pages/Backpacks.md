Backpacks are Slimefun [Items](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4/wiki/Items) which are simple ways to store items in a portable chest.

## Small Backpack
1 Row (9 Slots) of inventory.

## Backpack
2 Rows (18 Slots) of inventory.

## Large Backpack
3 Rows (27 Slots) of inventory.

## Woven Backpack
4 Rows (36 Slots) of inventory.

## Gilded Backpack
5 Rows (45 Slots) of inventory.

## Radiant Backpack
6 Rows (56 Slots) of inventory.
Equivilent to a double chest.
